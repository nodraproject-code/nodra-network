## 4. Early Support Allocation

**Early Support Allocation** is a limited, documented and controlled support path for individuals or collaborators who may want to support NODRA development directly.

This model does not require a PancakeSwap pool.

### Possible Flow

1. A supporter contacts NODRA through an official channel
2. NODRA shares the Early Support Policy and risk notice
3. The supporter confirms understanding of risks
4. The supporter provides a compatible BNB Chain wallet address
5. If accepted, NODRA sends tokens from the Treasury Wallet
6. The transaction hash is recorded for transparency

### Treasury Allocation

- **Early Support Allocation base:** 10,000,000 NODRA
- **Initial test transfer:** 1 NODRA
- **Current Treasury balance:** 10,000,001 NODRA

## 5. PancakeSwap Liquidity Pilot

NODRA may later evaluate a limited PancakeSwap Liquidity Pilot using the dedicated NODRA Liquidity Wallet.

### Potential Initial Model Under Review

- **NODRA amount:** 100,000 NODRA
- **BNB amount:** approximately 0.04 BNB

### Important Clarifications

- **This pool has NOT been created yet**
- DEX liquidity is **NOT active** at this stage
- Trading is **NOT officially active** at this stage
- Any future liquidity would be **experimental and limited**
- The purpose would be market access/testing, **not a promise of profit**

### How PancakeSwap Pricing Works

In PancakeSwap, price is defined by the ratio between NODRA and BNB provided to the pool.

**Example:**
If a pool is created with 100,000 NODRA and 0.04 BNB, PancakeSwap will calculate the initial price based on that ratio. The price will then change automatically based on buy/sell activity and available liquidity.

**Important:** This is not controlled by NODRA Network. PancakeSwap AMM (Automated Market Maker) determines price through market mechanics.
