This document defines how NODRA could manage a future limited liquidity on PancakeSwap in a transparent and prudent manner.

- NODRA does not guarantee value or trading volume
- Transparent documentation would precede and follow any action

## 5. How PancakeSwap Pricing Works

**Key Principle:**
PancakeSwap does not allow NODRA Network to set an arbitrary price manually.

**Initial Price Determination:**
The initial price is determined by the ratio of assets added to the pool.

**Example:**
If a pool is created with 100,000 NODRA and 0.04 BNB:
- PancakeSwap interprets that ratio as the starting price
- 1 NODRA ≈ 0.0004 BNB (or ~0.001 USD at current BNB prices)

**Price Changes:**
The price will then change automatically based on:
- User buy orders
- User sell orders
- Available liquidity
- AMM (Automated Market Maker) mechanics

**Important:**
NODRA Network does not control the price. PancakeSwap's AMM algorithm determines price through market mechanics.

## 6. Liquidity Risks

**Limited Liquidity Volatility:**
Limited liquidity can create high volatility.

**Risks:**

- Price can move strongly with small buy orders
- Price can fall strongly with small sell orders
- Automated trading bots may interact with the pool
- Buyers may misunderstand the nature and depth of liquidity
- Low liquidity does not represent deep market demand
- Founder or project wallets should avoid actions that may appear like manipulation
- Price discovery may be unpredictable

**Risk Acknowledgment:**
Anyone interacting with a low-liquidity pool assumes these risks. NODRA Network is not responsible for price movements or trading losses.

## 7. Project Commitment

NODRA Network commits to:

- **No artificial trading:** NODRA should not use artificial trading activity, wash trading, or misleading volume creation
- **No manipulation:** Founder, Treasury and Liquidity wallets should not sell or remove liquidity without public documentation and explanation
- **Minimal testing:** Any test swap should be minimal and documented as a technical verification only
- **Transparency first:** Any liquidity action will be announced and explained before execution
- **No deception:** NODRA will not misrepresent liquidity depth, project status, or token value

## 8. Future Possible Actions (If Liquidity Pilot Is Approved)

**Before Creating Liquidity:**
1. Public announcement via official channels
2. Clear documentation of rationale
3. Risk notice for potential users
4. Transaction details published in advance
5. No surprise liquidity creation

**During Liquidity Operations:**
1. Project focus remains on Human Signal and community
2. Liquidity operations are administrative, not trading focus
3. No aggressive promotional activity
4. Transparency about project status

**If Removing Liquidity:**
1. Public announcement with explanation
2. Transaction hash published
3. No sudden disappearance of liquidity
