## SPECIFIC RISKS

### 1. Technical Risks

**System Design Complexity**
- Decentralized identity systems are complex and difficult to implement correctly
- Reputation systems can be gamed, manipulated, or attacked
- Scaling to many participants introduces technical challenges
- Privacy and security create competing design tensions

**Security Risks**
- Cryptographic systems can have unforeseen vulnerabilities
- Smart contracts (if implemented) could contain critical bugs
- Private keys can be lost, stolen, or compromised
- No security guarantees despite best efforts

**Data Risks**
- Data loss is possible despite safeguards
- Privacy can be compromised by chain analysis or forensics
- Immutable ledgers cannot be corrected or revoked
- Data cannot be deleted from distributed systems

**Integration Risks**
- External platforms may change APIs or policies
- Integrations depend on third parties
- Centralized dependencies create single points of failure
- Interoperability is not guaranteed

---

### 2. Regulatory & Legal Risks

**Uncertain Legal Status**
- Regulatory treatment of tokens is unclear and jurisdiction-dependent
- Classification as a "security" could trigger strict regulations
- "Utility token" classification is contested by regulators
- Laws are evolving and may be applied retroactively

**Compliance Uncertainty**
- Legal requirements vary significantly by country
- United States (SEC, CFTC, FinCEN) has unclear rules
- European Union (MiCA) has strict requirements
- Other jurisdictions have varying levels of restriction

**Potential Legal Consequences**
- Project could be shut down by regulators
- Operators could face legal liability
- Participants could face tax or regulatory consequences
- Securities fraud charges possible (if misrepresented)

**Jurisdictional Conflicts**
- Different countries have conflicting regulations
- Compliance in one jurisdiction may violate another
- No legal entity exists to navigate complexity
- Decentralization doesn't exempt from laws

**Volatility**
- Cryptocurrency prices swing 20%+ daily

**Liquidity Risk**
- Tokens may not be tradeable even if created
- Exchanges may delist or refuse to list

**Irreversibility**
- Cryptocurrency transactions are permanent

**Wallet & Key Risks**
- Lost private keys = permanently lost funds
- Stolen keys = stolen funds
- Hacking incidents are frequent
- Exchange hacks have frozen user funds
